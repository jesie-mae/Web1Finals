DxS

SEVENTEEN's Sub-unit

PROFILE

History

On November 6, 2025, Pledis Entertainment announced that DK and Seungkwan of Seventeen would debut as a new sub-unit, making it the fifth sub-unit of the band. The two previously promoted as part of the unit group BSS, together with Hoshi, who recently enlisted in the military. In December, the pair released a cover of Bruno Mars and Lady Gaga's "Die with a Smile", and on December 17, the trailer for their debut extended play (EP) was released, revealing its title and release date, and on December 23, official photos for their album was released.

Background

DK X Seungkwan, also known as DxS is a special sub-unit of South Korean boy band Seventeen, consisting of vocal team members DK and Seungkwan. On January 12, SEVENTEEN’s DxS released their unit debut mini album 'Serenade' along with a music video for the title track “Blue.”
